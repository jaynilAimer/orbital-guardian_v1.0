# 🛰️ Orbital Guardian AI

## Advanced Space Situational Awareness & Planetary Defense Intelligence Platform

Orbital Guardian AI is a research-oriented Space Situational Awareness (SSA) and Planetary Defense Intelligence System designed to monitor, analyze, and visualize the dynamic environment surrounding Earth.

The platform combines asteroid intelligence, orbital analytics, satellite monitoring, collision-risk assessment, threat prioritization, and interactive visualization into a unified intelligence dashboard. Its objective is to transform publicly available space data into actionable insights that support research, education, risk analysis, and awareness of potential threats within near-Earth space.

---

# Mission

To democratize access to space intelligence by providing a powerful, interactive, and research-grade platform capable of monitoring Near-Earth Objects (NEOs), tracking satellites, analyzing orbital behavior, and assessing potential collision risks.

---

# Vision

To evolve into a comprehensive planetary defense ecosystem that leverages modern analytics, visualization technologies, and scientific methodologies to improve global awareness of space-based threats and orbital activities.

---

# Why Orbital Guardian AI?

The number of satellites, space debris objects, and near-Earth asteroids continues to increase rapidly.

Modern space operations require:

* Continuous monitoring
* Threat intelligence
* Collision prediction
* Orbital analytics
* Data-driven decision support

Orbital Guardian AI addresses these challenges by integrating scientific data sources with modern visualization and analytical techniques.
